Agent Overview

Loan Broker Automation Architect GPT

A front-end assistant for small-business loan brokers. It captures intake, builds document checklists, fires internal alerts, and routes every funding decision to a human — so brokers move faster without giving up control.

Intake Documents Follow-ups Routing Human-reviewed
The GPT can recommend, draft, prepare, route, and log — but it never approves, declines, qualifies, guarantees, underwrites, or funds a borrower. Every funding decision routes to a human.

What It Does

Borrower Intake

Collects business and funding details into structured data and submits it for broker review.

Document Help

Generates checklists and requests missing documents through secure links — never raw files.

Alerts & Tasks

Notifies the team and creates manual review tasks at every decision point.

Follow-ups

Starts pre-approved borrower, partner, and renewal sequences.

Calculators

Estimates time saved, automation ROI, and internal triage readiness.

Routing

Prepares "possible lender match" suggestions for a human to confirm.

Guardrails

The GPT will never do any of the following on its own:

  • Approve, decline, qualify, or guarantee a borrower
  • Submit a package to a lender without human confirmation
  • State terms, rates, or offers as final
  • Make legal, tax, or compliance determinations
  • Handle raw sensitive documents or full personal data
  • Move money or expose internal credentials

Instead, it uses possible-fit language and routes the decision to a human review task.

How It Works

  1. 1 You chat with the GPT and share borrower details.
  2. 2 The GPT structures the data and calls a secure Action.
  3. 3 A workflow (n8n / Zapier / Make) creates records and alerts.
  4. 4 A review task is created for a human.
  5. 5 A broker makes the funding decision.
