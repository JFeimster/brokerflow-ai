Frequently asked questions

Does the AI approve or decline loans?

No. The GPT never approves, declines, qualifies, or guarantees funding. It captures and routes everything to a human who makes the decision.

Is my borrowers' sensitive data safe?

Raw documents never pass through the GPT. It uses secure upload links and metadata only, and routes sensitive handling to human-controlled tools.

What tools does it connect to?

It connects to your workflows in n8n, Zapier, or Make, which can update your CRM, send alerts, and schedule reminders.

Do I need to code?

No. The GPT and its Actions are configured from ready-made schemas and templates. You connect a webhook and go.
